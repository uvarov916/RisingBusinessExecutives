Peer Mentors

Be a Volunteer

The team at Rising Business Executives Inc. is always looking to bring
on Peer Mentors interested in helping out high school students!

Before applying, we ask that you review the details below. If you have
any questions, pleas email us and we'll be sure to get back to you.

Peer Mentors Are

  • High-achieving college undergraduate students interested in hosting
    hands-on workshops, building lifelong relationships with high school
    students, and assisting those in need
  • Able to dedicate one hour a week for internal meetings, plus time
    (as needed) to assist the mentee
  • Minimum cumulative GPA of 3.0
  • Pursuing undergraduate degree degree in Business, Economics,
    Mathematics, or Computer Science(not limited)
  • Past experience in mentoring, tutoring, assisting others is a definite plus
